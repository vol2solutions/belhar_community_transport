Good Day and Welcome to the best Community Drivers Group
To our New and Existing Clients

As with any group we have RULES and Guidelines
Our Drivers are dedicated to Serve but due to Obligations towards the group the below Are Not Permitted:

  • No Private Trips (our Drivers are not allowed to take Private Trips)
  • No Lift Clubs (We Do not Do Lift Clubs)
  • We Do not Rush (Your Safety is our responsibility). If you are late we will try to get you there on time, but not at the risk of safety.

Guidelines

Booking of rides

  • Please provide full address and contact person when booking for someone else.
  • Bookings for the next day must be done before 20:30.
  • Please respect our vehicles and keep them clean.
  • For groups larger than 5, inform the driver beforehand.
  • Drivers wait up to 5 minutes only if busy.
  • Cancel before your booked time.
  • Immediate payment required for bank transfers.
  • Please be ready in high-risk areas for safety reasons.
  • Some areas are restricted — please understand.
  • Please report any harassment.

Remember we are here to assist you, our loyal clients.

If you cancel a next-day booking on the morning of the trip, you are responsible for paying half the fare.

If you have a complaint (we hope not) or a compliment (we hope you do), feel free to inform us — we enjoy interacting with our clients.
