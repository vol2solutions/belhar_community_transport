Booking a ride is as easy as 1, 2, 3

Getting a ride with Belhar Community Transport is quick and simple. Just follow these 3 easy steps and you're on your way!

1. Fill in Your Details

Enter your pickup location, drop-off, number of passengers, and time.

2. Submit Your Request

Click the WhatsApp button to copy your request and open WhatsApp.

3. Paste in the Group

Paste your request into the WhatsApp group and a driver will respond.
